🧠 SECTION 1: BASIC PROJECT INFO
🔹 1.1 Your SaaS Idea

What are you building?

👉 MoneyFlow AI is a smart finance tracking application designed for small business owners and individuals to track income, expenses, and recurring payments while gaining intelligent insights into their financial behavior.

👉 Users can add transactions in 2–3 taps, view real-time dashboards, and receive smart alerts and automation for better financial decisions.

🌍 1.2 Target Market

✔ India (Primary)
✔ Future: India + Gulf

Why this market?

Large number of small business owners
Many still use notebooks or Excel
High demand for simple finance tracking apps
Mobile-first users (Android heavy market)
⏱ 1.3 Timeline

✔ 3–4 days (MVP + Advanced Features)

Why?

Fast development using Flutter + NestJS
Cursor AI used for rapid implementation
Focus on core features first, then enhancements
💰 1.4 Budget

✔ Preference: Balanced (low cost + good quality)
